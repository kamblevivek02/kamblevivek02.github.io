# About Me

I am a seasoned cybersecurity professional with over 15 years of experience across governance, risk, compliance, security operations, and project leadership. I help organizations turn security into a business enabler — not a bottleneck.

My career spans **telecommunications, financial services, and consulting**, giving me a broad perspective on how to balance risk, regulation, and operational reality. I've led ISO 27001 certification programs, built third-party risk management (TPRM) frameworks from scratch, directed incident response, and embedded security into product development lifecycles.

I am **CISSP and ISO 27001 Lead Implementer certified**, with PMP and ACP credentials that allow me to bridge the gap between technical security and program delivery. Whether I'm advising leadership on risk posture, conducting deep-dive audits, or collaborating with engineers on secure-by-design architecture, my focus is always on **practical, measurable outcomes**.

Currently based in the **Netherlands** with a valid **Highly Skilled Migrant (HSM) visa**, I am open to roles in GRC, security operations, project management, AI governance, and risk advisory — across permanent, contract, or freelance engagements.

Beyond work, I practice **Vipassana meditation**, which sharpens my focus, resilience, and clarity in high-pressure environments — qualities I bring to every team and project I support.
