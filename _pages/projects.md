# Projects

## Project 1: ISO 27001 Certification Program (Telecommunications)

**Role:** Lead / Program Manager  
**Sector:** Telecommunications / Critical Infrastructure

Led the end-to-end ISO 27001 certification initiative for a major telecommunications operator, covering 3 major business functions. Managed the full lifecycle — gap assessment, policy development, control implementation, internal audits, and external certification audit.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Achieved **zero material findings** for 3 consecutive surveillance audits
- Developed **25+ security policies** aligned with ISO 27001 Annex A
- Established an enterprise-wide risk register with treatment plans and ownership
- Achieved **95% audit finding closure rate** within SLAs
